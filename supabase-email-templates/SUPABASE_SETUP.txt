SUPABASE SETUP

1. Open Supabase Dashboard.
2. Go to Authentication -> URL Configuration.
3. Add this Redirect URL:
   xpandlimits://auth/callback
4. Go to Authentication -> Email Templates.
5. Paste confirm-signup.html into Confirm signup.
6. Paste reset-password.html into Reset password.
7. Keep your app scheme in app.json as:
   "scheme": "xpandlimits"

NOTES
- Sign up now sends emailRedirectTo = xpandlimits://auth/callback
- Forgot password now sends redirectTo = xpandlimits://auth/callback
- The app now opens a reset-password screen after a recovery link
- After entering a new password, the app calls supabase.auth.updateUser({ password })
